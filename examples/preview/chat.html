How does SEAL handle forms compared to regular HTML?
2:14 PM

SEAL forms are dramatically simpler for both humans and AI agents.

In SEAL, you write:

seal 
Form #signup action="/register" method=POST
  Input #email type=email label="Email" required=true
  Input #pass  type=password label="Password" required=true
  Button #submit type=submit variant=primary width=full
    "Create Account"

The SEAL renderer automatically:

  • Generates semantic HTML with correct ARIA roles
  • Adds data-seal-actions attributes for AI discovery
  • Wires up client-side validation
  • Produces a structured AI manifest for automation
